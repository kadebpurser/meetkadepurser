The Gathering of Israel

What Is It?

The Gathering of Israel is a profound and significant event in Christian theology, including the unification and return of God's covenant people. This ongoing process is made possible only through Restored Godly Power found in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

Scriptural Foundation

  1. Key Scriptures
    • Isaiah 11:12 - "He will set up a banner for the nations, and will assemble the outcasts of Israel, and gather together the dispersed of Judah from the four corners of the earth."
    • Jeremiah 31:10 - "Hear the word of the Lord, O ye nations, and declare it in the isles afar off, and say, He that scattered Israel will gather him, and keep him, as a shepherd doth his flock."
    • Matthew 24:31 - "And he shall send his angels with a great sound of a trumpet, and they shall gather together his elect from the four winds, from one end of heaven to the other."
    • 1 Nephi 22:25: "And he gathereth his children from the four quarters of the earth; and he numbereth his sheep, and they know him; and there shall be one fold and one shepherd; and he shall feed his sheep, and in him they shall find pasture."
    • 3 Nephi 16:5: "And then will I gather them in from the four quarters of the earth; and I will fulfill the covenant which the Father hath made unto all the people of the house of Israel."
    • 3 Nephi 21:1 - "And verily I say unto you, I give unto you a sign, that ye may know the time when these things shall be about to take place—that I shall gather in, from their long dispersion, my people, O house of Israel, and shall establish again among them my Zion."
    • Doctrine and Covenants 110:11 - "After this vision closed, the heavens were again opened unto us; and Moses appeared before us, and committed unto us the keys of the gathering of Israel from the four parts of the earth, and the leading of the ten tribes from the land of the north."
  2. Modern Teachings
    • President Russell M. Nelson - "The gathering of Israel is the most important thing taking place on earth today. Nothing else compares in magnitude, nothing else compares in importance, nothing else compares in majesty."
    • Elder Jeffrey R. Holland - “The gathering of Israel is not just something to be read about in the scriptures, it is something to be a part of."
    • Elder Dieter F. Uchtdorf - “The Lord has a great work for us to do. Every man, woman, and child has been called to help in the divine effort to gather Israel."
